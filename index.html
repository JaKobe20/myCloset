Why This App is Awesome

Summary of the app's awesomeness

Header

This app allows you to completely virtualize your wardrobe. Store all of your clothing items anyway you want. Share your wardrobe with your friends. Receive recommendations based on the type of clothes you love the most. Take pictures of yourself in your clothes, so that you can view what you look like in them when you are away from home.
